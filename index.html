Traffic Protocols

Learn about safe driving practices, traffic rules, and how to stay safe on the road.

Why Ace is Important

Ace provides a real-time dash camera feed and security alert system that ensures safer roads by proactively detecting hazards, monitoring traffic violations, and providing instant alerts. The system promotes awareness of traffic rules and helps avoid accidents.

Safety Tips for Drivers

  • Always maintain a safe distance from other vehicles.
  • Adhere to speed limits and avoid reckless driving.
  • Stay focused and avoid distractions like mobile phones while driving.
  • Regularly check the condition of your vehicle's tires, brakes, and lights.
  • Use your indicators when changing lanes or making turns.

Using Ace's Features

Ace offers real-time monitoring, incident reporting, and integrated maps to ensure a safer driving experience. Simply access the system through your dashboard to view live feeds, submit reports, or navigate using our detailed traffic maps.
